WELCOME TO ST.TERESA OF CALCUTTA SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL

 

Background: St. Teresa of Calcutta Senior Secondary school was established in 2016 and is a product of Mother Teresa Nursery and primary school which was started as a center for disadvantaged children and war affected children. It is an initiative of the Little Sisters of Mary Immaculate of Gulu. It was registered as a local NGO in 2004 and in 2005; other children were brought from different places, for example, Oscar Olal who was brought by a soldier. In 2006 a vocational section was opened to cater for the war affected mothers and the disabled persons. In 2007 a Nursery section and Primary section was opened to cater for the disabled and child mothers’ children to study from while their mothers are studying in the Vocational section the trades of Catering and Tailoring. It has since developed up to secondary level; the pioneer lot sat UCE in 2019.St. Teresa of Calcutta SS is a mixed Catholic privately founded Secondary School by Little Sisters of Mary Immaculate of Gulu(LISMIG).

                                                                                                         Ownership
The school is owned by the little sisters of Mary Immaculate of Gulu by a sole founded Director; Rev. Sister Hellen Lamunu (Head teacher of Sacred Heart Girls’ SS-Gulu).
